Our History

Fuson was originally created as an expression of the founder's passion for the production world. They decided to create a project that would allow them to not only achieve their goals but become tangible evidence of that. The greatest satisfaction for them is seeing Fuson grow, and that’s why they carry the sense of quality with them that is necessary to build a project akin to this. If you're looking for someone to seriously care about your next event, you’ve found what you’ve been looking for.

Our Experience

While the company was founded in 2021 the founders have been working in the production world long before the company's founding. Ben got his start in the 6th grade working miscellaneous production volunteering jobs at churches learning about what it takes to make an event successful. While Bentley got his start working different events during his freshman year of high school. With over a decade of experience just between the founders, we have a team ready to deliver your next successful event. From Weddings, Corporate Events, Private Parties, and Production sectors of the entertainment industry Fuson Entertainment has you covered.
