What We Do

At Fuson Entertainment, our mission is to create exceptional events that exceed our client’s expectations and leave a positive impact on the people and communities around us. We are dedicated to delivering top-quality service and innovative solutions that reflect our client’s vision and values. By working collaboratively with our clients and partners, we strive to create memorable and meaningful experiences that engage, inspire, and entertain. We are committed to conducting business with the highest level of integrity, professionalism, and respect for all stakeholders, including our clients, employees, vendors, and the environment. We believe that every event has the potential to make a difference, and we are dedicated to helping our clients achieve their goals and realize the full potential of their events.

Who We Are

Fuson Entertainment was originally founded by Bentley Dombrow and Ben Lovelace. Fuson was created to formalize the work they had been doing together and to bring the services they offered to a broader audience. With a passion for music and audio-visual technology alike, the two decided to combine their skills and began working together as a team. Since then, it has grown past anything that they had first imagined for it.
